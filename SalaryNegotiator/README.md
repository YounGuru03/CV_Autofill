# Salary Negotiator

Calculate fair salary ranges based on role, experience, location, and other factors. Get negotiation tips and email templates.

## Features

- Salary calculation based on multiple factors
- Total compensation breakdown
- Percentile ranges (25th, 50th, 75th)
- Negotiation tips and strategies
- Email template generator
- Location and company size adjustments

## Usage

1. Open index.html
2. Enter job details
3. Click "Calculate Fair Salary"
4. Review salary ranges and total comp
5. Use negotiation tips and email template

## Factors Considered

- Job title and level
- Years of experience
- Geographic location
- Education level
- Company size
- Bonus and equity estimates

## Note

Estimates are based on general market data. Actual salaries vary by company, industry, and individual qualifications.
